Features

Activities you'll love.

Aim of these activities is to sensitize the students about the importance of celebrating the Earth Day. Extending upon to this aim, we can mention few of the objectives as below.

Slogan Writing Competition

Slogan is that catchy line which gets everyone’s attention and conveys a huge message in a small fraction. An effective slogan remains in the mind for long time, and craft an influential impact. Slogans have proved to be effectual in awareness and sensitization on any issue.

Quiz

In the words of Mark Goulston “The purpose of the quiz is not to shame or embarrass anyone, but to make sure everyone is one the same page”. Conduction of quiz is there just to make oneself aware about the knowledge that one possess.

Poster making

“Creativity is allowing yourself to make mistakes. Design is knowing which ones to keep”-anonymous. The quote mentioned above clearly depicts the importance of pictorial representation in areas of broader importance.

Tug of War

In the words of Robert G.Allen “Creating success is a tug of war between your mind and your heart, your fears and your dreams. If you fears win, you lose.

Dumb charades

“Actions speak louder than words”-this very quote has been imbibed to our memories since beginning .Apart from being a fun activity the main purpose of this event is to: 1.) Spread awareness about the importance of actions that are far more important than just mere speeches and the words.

Model making competition

This very event is based on the principle of making the “best out of the waste”, where participants will be given junk materials and they will have to unwound the loopholes of their mind and create the useful material which will help us for our future survival.

Tree plantation

The deteriorated condition of the environment has not been hidden, to put a halt to sources that accelerates the detrimental activities is unavoidable these days but the measures to resist them seems quite possible and tree plantation is one of them. Thus it is basically done to promote the logo “Go green go clean”.

What are the Themes?

1. Carbon Emissions and Climate Change
2. Prevention is better than cure-Elaborate in context of Mother Earth.
3. Relief Chain Logistics in Natural Disasters
4. Tsunami Early Warning system
5. The role of bio-energy in a climate-changing world
6. Low impact development for storm water management
7.  E-Waste Recycling and Management
8. Innovation in municipal waste collection systems
9. Recovery of valuable metals from waste printed circuit boards
10.   Use of greywater in production of mortar and concrete.
11. Cyber Security.
12. Augmented Reality V/S Virtual Reality
13. SCADA & Automation.

What are we looking for?

Adherence to the contest rules and regulations Style, Originality and Creativity
Alignment with the theme and clarity of thought
Presentation Style
Quality of content and passion for the subject
Note : Each point will be graded on a scale of 1-10
